The story of the month

Three growth moments

i.
The lightbulb on remainders
Aaira had a real lightbulb moment when she realised that remainders become fractions because all groups must be equal. Once she saw it, the connection to fractions clicked everywhere.
ii.
Owning her own learning
When her assessment approached, Aaira asked to switch focus from English to Maths revision. A small choice on the surface, but a big sign that she's starting to lead her own learning.
iii.
From quiet to confident
Across four practice deliveries of her OREO speech, her volume grew, her gestures appeared, and her emphasis sharpened. By the final delivery, she was performing, not reading.
